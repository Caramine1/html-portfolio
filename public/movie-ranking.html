The Best Movies According to Caramine

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Home Alone 1

Home alone is an american comedy family movie. funny and entertaining.

zootopia

zootopia is an animated film. Sure to speak to kids and grown-ups alike, Zootopia unfolds a poignant lesson about how prejudice can hurt people, but also how it can be overcome. And it does all this in a wonderfully fun film.

Ratatouille

This is a film about a rat who becomes a chef in Paris. But it's so much more than that. It's about what a great artist can be, where a great artist can come from, it's about food and how to savour it.
